# 💻 Types of Computers

## 🖥️ Desktop Computers
- Stationary devices used at a fixed location
- High performance compared to portable devices
- Easy to upgrade and maintain

## 💻 Laptop Computers
- Portable and lightweight
- Powered by a battery
- Limited upgrade options compared to desktops

## 📱 Mobile Devices
- Includes smartphones and tablets
- Highly portable and easy to use
- Touch-based interface

## 🖧 Servers
- Designed to provide services to other computers (clients)
- High performance and reliability
- Used in networks and data centers
