# ⚡ Power Supply Unit (PSU)

## 📌 Definition
The Power Supply Unit converts AC power from the wall into DC power used by computer components.

## 🧠 Key Functions
- Supplies power to motherboard
- Supplies power to CPU, GPU, storage devices

## ⚙️ Types
- ATX Power Supply (common in desktops)

## 🔥 Important Notes (Exam Focus)
- Measured in Watts (W)
- Provides different voltage rails (3.3V, 5V, 12V)

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting
- PC not turning on → Check PSU cables
- Random shutdowns → Possible power issue
