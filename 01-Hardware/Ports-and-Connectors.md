# 🔌 Ports and Connectors

## 📌 Definition
Ports are physical interfaces on a computer used to connect external devices.

## 🧠 Common Ports

- USB (Keyboard, Mouse, Flash Drive)
- HDMI (Video & Audio Output)
- DisplayPort (High-quality video output)
- Ethernet (Network connection)
- Audio Jack (Headphones, Speakers)
- VGA (Older video connection)

## ⚙️ Types of Ports
- Input Ports
- Output Ports
- Input/Output Ports

## 🔥 Important Notes (Exam Focus)
- USB is the most common port
- HDMI supports both audio and video
- Ethernet is used for wired networking

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting
- No display → Check HDMI/DisplayPort cable
- No internet → Check Ethernet cable connection
