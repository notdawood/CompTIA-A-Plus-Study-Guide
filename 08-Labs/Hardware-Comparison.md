# 🧪 Lab: HDD vs SSD Comparison

## 🎯 Objective
Understand the difference between HDD and SSD in real-world usage.

## 🛠️ Task
Compare HDD and SSD based on the following:

- Speed
- Price
- Durability
- Power consumption

## 🧠 What You Need to Do
Write your conclusion explaining which one is better and why.

## 💡 Example:
SSD is better for performance because it is significantly faster, more durable, and has no moving parts.
